About CANTIFY

The story, the team, the mission

🎯

Our Mission

CANTIFY was born from a simple frustration: CHARUSAT students lose 15–20 minutes of every lunch break standing in canteen queues. We built this platform to give that time back — so students can pre-order, walk straight to the counter and use their break for rest, socialising or that last-minute revision.
